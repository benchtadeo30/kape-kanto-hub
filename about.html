About Us

Our Story

A concept we came up with for our class.

📌 Note: Kanto Kape Hub does not exist as a real business. This is a concept we created for our web development subject. All the details on this site — the menu, the prices, the address — are all part of our idea as a group.

We started with a simple question: what kind of coffee shop would we actually want to go to as students? Not the ones that are too fancy or too expensive, but somewhere chill and affordable.

That is how we came up with Kanto Kape Hub. A small corner coffee shop where you can sit down, order something cheap, and just hang out. Nothing complicated about it.

We named it Kanto Kape because we imagined it being in a corner of the neighborhood — the kind of place that is easy to find and feels like it belongs there.

  • Fresh brewed coffee every morning — no reheated stuff
  • Beans sourced from Benguet and Sagada (that was the plan)
  • Prices starting at 59 pesos — affordable for students
  • Open daily 6AM to 10PM in our concept
  • Free Wi-Fi included so you can study or work
  • Freshly baked pastries every day
