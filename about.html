About Us

Our Story

A cozy coffee spot we kept imagining and building little by little.

📌 Note: Kanto Kape Hub is our idea of a neighborhood coffee spot. The menu, prices, and store details here are sample details we made for the brand while figuring out how we wanted the place to feel.

We started with a simple question: what kind of coffee shop would we actually want to go to as students? Not the ones that are too fancy or expensive, but somewhere chill and affordable.

That is how we came up with Kanto Kape Hub. A small corner coffee shop where you can sit down, order something cheap, and just hang out. Nothing too complicated.

We named it Kanto Kape because we imagined it being right at the corner of the neighborhood — easy to find and feels like it belongs there.

  • Fresh brewed coffee every morning, no reheated stuff
  • Beans we would love to source from Benguet and Sagada
  • Prices starting at ₱59 — affordable for students
  • Open daily 6AM to 10PM in our idea
  • Free Wi-Fi so you can study or work
  • Freshly baked pastries every day
